  • Overview

    During lockdown a large list of youtube links was dumped in our junior college whatsapp chat daily.
    By the second week it had become a hassle to keep track of our progress or refer to a previous topic.
    This website was created as a solution where over 450 different links are stored as iframes in
    a topicwise arrangement for easy access. The entire website as well as the curation, sorting
    and processing of links was done within a weekend using efficient and simple task automation
    and Google Sites as a website hosting platform.
