The Problem

Currently almost all storage can be divided into two main sub-categories, solid state and mechanical this means they have:

Limited Capacity

Currently buying a storage device locks you into its maximum capacity, i.e. if you buy a 4 GB SSD you can store a maximum of 4 GB of data on it.

Limited Scalability

Storage devices have information density, i.e. they take up a certain amount of volume in the computer for the amount of storage they provide. Currently to expand your storage you need to invest in more devices as well as more space to fit those
